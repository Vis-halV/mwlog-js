# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project aims to provide a respectful, constructive, and welcoming environment for everyone who participates.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful in language and tone
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements constructively
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and behavior rather than individuals
- Help keep discussions productive and inclusive

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or demeaning language
- Repeated disruption of project discussions
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private channel with the maintainers through the repository contact options or issue tracker as appropriate.
